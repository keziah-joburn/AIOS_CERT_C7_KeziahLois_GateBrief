# Support Touchpoints: Your Human + AI Support System

> Works two ways: **standalone** (you own and run your AIOS yourself) or **agency-managed** (an agency like Funnel Futurist set it up with you). Where this says "your agency," that means them, or you if you're solo.

> You're never alone in this. Here's every way you can get help, when to use each one, and how to book them.

---

## Your Support Layers

### Layer 1: AI Support (Available 24/7)

| Tool | When to Use | How |
|---|---|---|
| **Claude Code (this workspace)** | Questions about your strategy, offer, brand, or any docs | Just type your question |
| **AI Coach (Portal)** | Quick questions when browsing the portal | Chat widget at your agency's portal (if you have one) |
| **Unstuck Protocol** | When you're stuck and not sure which tool to use | Type: "Open the unstuck protocol" |

**Use AI first for:** Quick questions, drafting content, reviewing copy, understanding your docs, brainstorming, finding information.

---

### Layer 2: Scheduled Calls (Book as Needed)

| Call Type | What It's For | Duration | How to Book |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Catalyst Call** | Strategic check-in: review progress, set direction, remove roadblocks, celebrate wins | 30 min | Through your agency's portal or contact (if your engagement includes this, or your agency's equivalent) |
| **Technical Support Call** | Specific tech issue: workspace setup, platform questions, tool troubleshooting | 15 min | Through your agency's portal or Slack (if your engagement includes this) |
| **AIOS Hot Seat** | Deep dive on your AI workspace: skills, prompts, workflow optimization | 30 min | Through your agency's portal (if your engagement includes this, or your agency's equivalent) |
| **QBR (Quarterly Business Review)** | Big picture: metrics, strategy pivot assessment, next quarter planning | 45-60 min | Scheduled by your agency quarterly (if your engagement includes this) |

**When to book a call vs. ask AI:**
- AI can't see your screen: book a Technical Support Call
- You need a strategic decision, not information: book a Catalyst Call
- You want to learn new workspace skills: book a Hot Seat
- Everything else: ask Claude first

---

### Layer 3: Async Communication (Anytime)

| Channel | When to Use | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Slack** (if set up) | Quick questions, sharing updates, non-urgent issues | Same day |
| **Email** | Formal requests, document sharing, scheduling | 24-48 hours |
| **Review queue feedback** | Deliverable revisions (tell Claude "I don't like X") | Logged instantly, your agency acts within 48hrs (if agency-managed) |
| **GitHub Issues** (advanced) | Feature requests or bug reports on your workspace | Next business day |

---

## The Escalation Path

```
1. Ask Claude (instant)
   ↓ still stuck after 5 min?
2. Check the unstuck protocol (2 min)
   ↓ still stuck?
3. Message on Slack or email (same day response)
   ↓ need real-time help?
4. Book a Technical Support Call (15 min, next available)
   ↓ strategic, not technical?
5. Book a Catalyst Call (30 min)
```

---

## Call Rhythm by Phase

| Phase | Typical Call Cadence | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Foundation** | Weekly or biweekly | F6 review, offer refinement, setup |
| **Cyborg VA and AIOS** | Biweekly | Operator/VA placement, workspace optimization |
| **CRM and Monetization** | Biweekly | Pipeline setup, sequence review |
| **Distribution** | Monthly | Content review, ad performance |
| **Ongoing Success** | Monthly + Quarterly QBR | Metrics, strategy, scaling |

Your cadence adjusts based on how active you are. More activity means more frequent touchpoints. Coasting means monthly check-ins to keep momentum.

## After Every Call

Your agency (or you, if solo) logs:
- **Meeting summary** in `06_Communication/meeting_notes/`
- **Decisions made** in `06_Communication/decisions_log.md`
- **Action items** in `04_Customer_Journey/task_tracker.md`

You can always ask Claude: **"What was decided on our last call?"** and it pulls from the decisions log.
